How it works

Built for events you can't afford to lose.

Every event recorded the moment it happens — with zero latency between occurrence and inscription. No buffering, no batching, no delay.

Your service emits an event. Gbeye receives it, timestamps it server-side, and persists it instantly. The record exists before your response returns. status: received

Cryptographic proof attached to every event. Each payload is signed with HMAC-SHA256 at ingestion time, producing a unique fingerprint that cannot be forged or replicated without the original secret.

If the payload ever changes — even one character — the signature fails. Gbeye detects tampering so you don't have to.

Permanent history, visualized in real time. Query anytime from anywhere. Filter by actor, event type, status, or date range. Paginate through your entire event log without losing context.

Your events never expire. Access the analytics dashboard to see breakdowns, timelines, and a live feed of everything that has ever happened inside your service.
